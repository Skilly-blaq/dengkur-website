    Meet Our Artisans

    DengKur proudly supports talented African artisans who create authentic handmade crafts. Each artisan brings generations of cultural knowledge, creativity, and craftsmanship to every product they make.

    Bol Kun Tut Nyang

    Bol is a South Sudanese woodcarver from Akobo/Jonglei State who has been crafting traditional African sculptures for over 20 years. His carvings reflect wildlife, culture, and African storytelling.

    Nyajuok Deng Gabriel Gany

    Nyajuok specializes in Maasai beadwork and jewelry. Her colorful necklaces and bracelets celebrate East African traditions and empower women in her community.

    Deng Lual Mabor Tut

    Deng is an Ethiopian master basket weaver from Addis Ababa. Using natural fibers and traditional weaving techniques, he creates beautiful handmade baskets used in homes around the world.

    About DengKur

    DengKur is an online marketplace dedicated to connecting talented African artisans with customers across the globe. Our platform celebrates the richness of African culture through handmade crafts that tell stories of tradition, creativity, and heritage.

    We work directly with local artisans to ensure fair trade, sustainability, and authenticity in every product. From handwoven baskets to vibrant Ankara fabrics, each item on DengKur is crafted with passion and skill.

    Our mission is to empower African creators by giving them a global platform to showcase their work, earn sustainable income, and preserve cultural traditions.
